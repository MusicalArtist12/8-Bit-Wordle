Adafruit Metro clock speed:
    16 * 2^20 = 2^24 Hz

    59 ns per instruction

1200ns wait (how long the enable pin needs to be on for)

200ns wait (before reading Data)
(25 ns rise time + 140 pulse width)

SI units
M (mega) - 10^6, 2^20
k (kilo) - 10^3, 2^10

m (milli) - 10^-3, 2^-10
u (micro) - 10^-6, 2^-20
n (nano)  - 10^-9, 2^-30


E on 0 ns 
    wait, end at 100 ns
    read, end at 59 ns
E off
